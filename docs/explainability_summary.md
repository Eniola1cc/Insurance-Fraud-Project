# Model Explainability Summary

## Purpose

Explainability was added to make the Insurance Fraud Risk Prioritisation System more transparent and useful for business users.

Rather than only predicting whether a claim is fraudulent, the system helps explain why a claim may be considered high risk.

## Method Used

SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) was used to identify the features that contributed most strongly to fraud risk predictions.

## Key Benefits

- Helps investigators understand why a claim was flagged
- Supports transparent decision-making
- Improves trust in the model
- Identifies important fraud drivers
- Supports claim-level investigation prioritisation

## Business Interpretation

The explainability output allows fraud teams to review high-risk claims with supporting evidence, instead of relying only on a black-box model score.

This makes the solution more suitable for real-world fraud analytics and insurance investigation workflows.
