Business Context
Insurance companies process thousands of claims daily, but only a limited number can be manually reviewed for fraud. This leads to inefficiencies, missed fraud cases, and unnecessary workload on investigators.

Problem Statement
There is currently no intelligent system to prioritise which claims should be investigated first based on fraud risk.

Objective
To build a data-driven system that predicts the likelihood of fraud and ranks claims by risk, enabling investigators to focus on high-risk claims.

Key Business Questions
Which claims should be reviewed first?
How much fraud can be detected if only 10–20% of claims are reviewed?
What factors are most associated with fraudulent claims?
